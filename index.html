About - Singing

Gabe sings bass-baritone in opera, choral, and art song performances both alone and with many local ensembles. Those ensembles include NOISE (Northwest Opera In Schools, Etcetera), Harmonia Orchestra & Chorus, The Swedish Singers, Blessed Sacrament, and others. Previously, in Minnesota, Gabe performed with Minnesota Opera, Mixed Precipitation's Picnic Operetta, and Gilbert & Sullivan Light Opera Company.
