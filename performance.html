About

Gabe sings bass-baritone in opera, choral, and art song, both as a soloist and with ensembles around Seattle. Those include NOISE (Northwest Opera In Schools, Et cetera), Harmonia Orchestra & Chorus, The Swedish Singers, and Blessed Sacrament Parish.

Before Seattle, he sang in Minnesota with Minnesota Opera, Mixed Precipitation's Picnic Operetta, and the Gilbert & Sullivan Light Opera Company.
