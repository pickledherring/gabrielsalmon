About Gabriel Salmon

Gabriel Salmon is a bass-baritone based in Seattle who performs with the Swedish Choir of Seattle, Blessed Sacrament, and Harmonia. His vocal work spans both church music and concert repertoire throughout the Pacific Northwest. Previously in Minnesota, Gabriel performed with Minnesota Opera, Mixed Precipitation's Picnic Operetta, and Gilbert & Sullivan Light Opera Company. His voice is resonant and commanding, with a dark, velvety tone in the lower range that opens to a bright, clear upper register. Since relocating to Seattle in 2023, Gabriel has quickly integrated into the local music community.
